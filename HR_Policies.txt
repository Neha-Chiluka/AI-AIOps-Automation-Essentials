Maternity Leave Policy:
- We provide 16 weeks of paid leave for new mothers.
- An additional 4 weeks of unpaid leave may be requested.

Paternity Leave Policy:
- We provide 4 weeks of paid leave for new fathers or adoptive parents.

Remote Work Policy:
- Full-time employees may request to work from home up to 3 days per week.

Vacation Policy:
- Employees are entitled to 20 paid vacation days per year.
- Additional days accrue after 5 years of service.

Work Hours Policy:
- The standard workweek is 40 hours, Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.
- Flexible hours may be arranged with manager approval.
